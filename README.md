**Amazon Prime Video EDA**
**Overview**

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Amazon Prime Video’s dataset to understand content trends, 
viewer preferences, genre popularity, ratings, and release patterns. 
The analysis helps identify factors affecting audience engagement and platform growth.


**Objectives**

1-Analyze Movies vs TV Shows distribution
2-Identify popular genres and countries
3-Explore IMDb ratings and popularity trends
4-Understand viewer preferences and content performance


**Tools Used**

1-Python

2-Pandas

3-NumPy

4-Matplotlib

5-Seaborn

**Key Insights**

1-Movies dominate the platform, but TV Shows improve viewer retention.

2-Drama and Comedy are highly popular genres.

3-The USA and India produce the most content.

4-High popularity does not always mean high IMDb ratings.


**Conclusion**

The project provides valuable insights into audience preferences and content trends, helping Amazon Prime Video improve content strategy, audience engagement, and business growth.
